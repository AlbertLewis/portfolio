About Me

Hi! I'm Albert, a student at Worcester Polytechnic Institute (WPI). I am currently pursuing a B.S. and M.S. in Robotics Engineering with a Minor in Computer Science.
My interests are mainly in Artificial Intelligence, Controls, and Motion Planning, but I most enjoy myself when I am put in situations where I can tackle complex challenges, work in a team environment, acquire new skills and broaden my horizons. Although my interests are software oriented, I also love working with mechanical and electrical systems as seen from my projects below.

Outside of working, some of my hobbies include playing soccer, cooking, playing the violin, working out, and playing with cats.
